Value 1: Type of twist to do (0 to disable)
Value 2: The angle it will twist at (should be positive)
1 - Twists every beat
2 - Twists every second beat
3 - Twists every third beat
4 - Twists every fourth beat
5 - Twists on first and second beat, second half of third beat, and fourth beat
6 - Twists on third beat of each section only
7 - same as 5 but twice as slow
8 - Twists on second and fourth beat
